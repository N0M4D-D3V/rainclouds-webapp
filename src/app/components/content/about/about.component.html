RazaK

About the band

Atmospheric/Melodic/Post Black Metal band from Spain. It was formed in 2019 by Exar Kun (guitars) and Banshee (vocals).

The name of the band comes from Eragon book series.In them, appear some creatures with named 'Ra'zac'. The book describes them like this:

"They are the monsters in the dark, the dripping nightmares that haunt your race..."

Recording

About Dark Void Studios and Wildcrow Studios

Banshee has a home studio named Dark Void Studios. All releases are recorded and produced there. We use the best software and hardware we could find for make our music better.

Wildcrow Studios is where bands like Uten Hap or Wintercrown make their records. The men behind this is called Wildcrow, a trve norwegian black metal lover. Wildcrow takes care of the themes mastering.
